Frequently Asked Questions

We partner with a wide range of organizations including corporations, NGOs, foundations, educational institutions, government agencies, and individual philanthropists. What matters most is shared commitment to empowering girls and women in Liberia.

Partnership commitments vary based on the type of partnership. For corporate partnerships, we typically start at $5,000 annually. However, we also welcome in-kind contributions, volunteer support, and skills-based partnerships at various levels.

The partnership process typically takes 4-8 weeks from initial inquiry to formal agreement, depending on the complexity of the partnership. We work efficiently while ensuring thorough due diligence and alignment.

Partners receive regular impact reports including quantitative data, stories from the field, financial transparency, and program updates. Reporting frequency and format are customized based on partnership level and requirements.

Yes! We encourage partners to visit our program sites in Liberia. Site visits are arranged based on partnership level and can include meetings with beneficiaries, program staff, and community leaders.
