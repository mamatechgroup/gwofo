About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the team working to empower girls and women in Liberia

Our Story

Founded in 2010, the Girls and Women Foundation Liberia emerged from a deep-seated commitment to address the systemic barriers that prevent girls and women from reaching their full potential in Liberian society.

Our journey began with a small group of passionate advocates who witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by girls in accessing education and women in securing economic independence. Over the years, we have grown into a leading organization dedicated to creating lasting change through sustainable programs and strategic partnerships.

Today, we work across 15 counties in Liberia, implementing programs that have directly impacted over 7,000 individuals and indirectly benefited thousands more through community-wide initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower girls and women in Liberia through education, healthcare, economic opportunities, and advocacy, creating a society where every girl and woman can achieve her full potential and contribute meaningfully to national development.

Our Vision

A Liberia where girls and women have equal opportunities, access to quality education, economic independence, and participate fully in decision-making processes at all levels of society.

Our Objectives

  • Increase girls' school enrollment and retention rates
  • Provide vocational training for women
  • Improve access to healthcare services
  • Advocate for gender-responsive policies
  • Create economic empowerment opportunities
