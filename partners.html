Success Stories Through Partnership

Girls Education Program Expansion

In partnership with Liberia Ministry of Education

Through our collaboration with the Ministry of Education, we expanded our Girls Education Program to 10 additional schools, reaching 500 more girls with scholarships and educational support.

500+ Girls Reached
10 Schools
85% Retention Rate

Women Entrepreneurship Initiative

In partnership with Liberia Bank for Development

Our partnership with LBDI provided micro-loans and financial literacy training to 200 women entrepreneurs, resulting in 150 successful small businesses and increased household incomes.

200 Women Trained
150 Businesses Started
45% Income Increase
