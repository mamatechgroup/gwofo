Success Stories Through Partnership

COVID-19 Household Food Support Program

In partnership with Caritas Cape Palmas and World Food Programme

Through our collaboration with Caritas Cape Palmas and WFP, we implemented the Government of Liberia's Household Food Support Program (COHFSP) in Grand Gedeh County during the COVID-19 pandemic, providing vital food assistance to vulnerable households.

$31,000 Grant Amount
2020-2021 Implementation Period

Women and Youth Land Ownership Project

In partnership with Forum Civ Liberia

Our 4-year collaboration with Forum Civ Liberia focuses on "Strengthening Local Structures to Increase Awareness and Sustain Interventions that Promotes Women and Youth Land Ownership and Decision-making in Land Administration and Management" in Grand Gedeh County.

$104,981.93 Total Grant
4 Years Project Duration
2022-2025 Implementation Period

Women's Rights and Health Advocacy

In partnership with Medica Liberia and UNDP

Completed a 2-month project on "Women Inclusion within County Development Agenda to Eliminate All Forms of Violence & Harmful Practices against Women and Girls & Increase Access to Sexual Reproductive Health Rights" in Grand Gedeh County.

$5,000 Grant Amount
2 Months Project Duration
2022 Implementation Year

Rural Women Livelihoods Empowerment

GWOFO-Liberia Flagship Program

Our ongoing flagship program "Rural Women Livelihoods Empowerment Programs" trains women in various skills, home economy, and petit businesses to contribute to household development, benefiting over 30 young and elderly women in Zwedru and its environs.

30+ Women Beneficiaries
2016-Present Ongoing Program
Multiple Skills Training Areas
